Ever eaten a bowl of stew only to be left with a 1% serving you can't eat and can't stack?

UI Tweaks slaps the hard numbers directly on your HUD so you can actually min-max your bowl of stew.

Features

Environment Widget

A small overlay showing the in-game time, temperature, and your coordinates. Position, size, format, and background opacity are all configurable. Supports VTML and variable placeholders.

Status Tooltips

Inspired by Extended HUD Info.

Numeric readouts on your HUD bars:

  • HP — current / max health above the health bar
  • Satiety — current / max satiety above the satiety bar
  • Hunger rate — your current hunger drain rate
  • Temporal Stability — personal stability and location stability

Each tooltip can be toggled and repositioned independently.

Quick Search

Inspired by Satisfactory's quick-search.

Press N (configurable) to search handbook entries by name without opening the handbook. Results filter as you type.

Calculator

Quick Search also evaluates arithmetic expressions (+, -, *, /). Useful when mixing alloys and you need exact metal ratios — or when you need to know how many planks you need and can't be bothered to alt-tab out.

Configuration

Generated on first launch. Position, size, format, and background style are configurable per element.
